• About

    half step - learn music one step at a time

    We are half step. Music practice can be unfocused, filled with self-doubt, lonely, and disconnected from your goals and dreams. half step helps you climb higher by providing bite-sized tasks, near-peer support, and progress tracking. half step is an iOS app that breaks down music practice into fun steps that will take you to the next level.
